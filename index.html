Syntax

* + - / ^ with scalars and named objects, plus det(A), inv(A), trans(A), rref(M), eig(A). Implicit multiplication works too: 2A, A(B+B).

The Plane view animates 2×2 matrices and 2‑vectors — products like A*B*v play stage by stage, right to left. Products and rref also get numeric step‑by‑step animations in the Steps view.
