Syntax reference

* + - / ^ with scalars and named objects (B^4, A^-1, 2A + B), plus det(), inv(), trans(), rref(), eig(). Implicit multiplication works: 2A, A(B+B).

Inline matrices work anywhere: [1 2; 3 4] * [5; 6] — rows separated by ;. The Sandbox tab is built around them.

Graph view animates 2×2 matrices and 2‑vectors (products play right to left). Matrix view works for any size and shows the arithmetic with color-coded steps.

Whiteboard view — driven by the Expression box on the left, with all your objects. Inline matrices work there too: [1 2] is a row, [1; 2] a column, [1 2; 3 4] a 2×2 (; separates rows). The equation previews here as you type; press Go to step through it.
